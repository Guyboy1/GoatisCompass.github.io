# 🧭 Goatis Health Compass

A Goatis-style take on the classic political compass — but for health, diet, and lifestyle.

Inspired by the structure of the SapplyValues political compass quiz, this app uses a series of weighted questions to place you on a 2D grid representing your habits and tendencies.

---

## 📍 The Compass

Your result is determined across two axes:

### ➡️ Horizontal (Diet Spectrum)
- **Left:** Omnivore  
- **Right:** Carnivore  

### ⬆️ Vertical (Lifestyle Spectrum)
- **Bottom:** Goy Slop  
- **Top:** Primal Nutt  

These axes are descriptive, not moral — every quadrant has trade-offs.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

- Answer a set of structured questions  
- Each response contributes to axis scoring  
- Final position is calculated and plotted on the compass  

The system is inspired by traditional political compass-style quizzes, adapted for health and lifestyle interpretation.

---

## 🧭 Interpretation

This compass is about **tendencies**, not “good vs bad”.

|                | **Primal Nutt (Top)** | **Goy Slop (Bottom)** |
|----------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| **Carnivore**  | Structured, high-discipline approach | Unstructured, instinct-driven habits |
| **Omnivore**   | Balanced and varied approach | Flexible, convenience-driven habits |

> Extremes in any direction may come with downsides — the goal is awareness, not labeling.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

- Visualise your health and lifestyle patterns  
- Compare results with others  
- Encourage reflection without prescribing a “correct” answer
