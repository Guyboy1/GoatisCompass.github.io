# 🧭 Goatis Health Compass

A TikTok-inspired spin on the classic political compass — but for your health habits.

Built around the style and energy of Goatis, this app maps how you eat, train, sleep, and live onto a chaotic but insightful 2D grid.

---

## 📍 The Compass

Your result is placed on a grid with two axes:

### ➡️ Horizontal (Diet Spectrum)
- **Left:** Omnivore  
- **Right:** Carnivore  

### ⬆️ Vertical (Lifestyle Energy)
- **Bottom:** Goy Slop  
- **Top:** Primal Nutt  

---

## 🧠 Features

- Swipe-style questions (fast + TikTok-like)
- Scores your:
  - Diet 🥩
  - Fitness 💪
  - Sleep 😴
  - Lifestyle ⚡
- Generates a **shareable compass result**

---

## 🔥 Archetypes

|                | **Primal Nutt (Top)** | **Goy Slop (Bottom)** |
|----------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| **Carnivore**  | Disciplined, intense, high-performance | Meat diet, zero structure |
| **Omnivore**   | Balanced, optimized lifestyle | Surviving, not thriving |

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Not meant to be serious or preachy — just a fun way to:
- Visualize your habits
- Compare with friends
- Maybe improve a few things

---

## 🚀 Tech Stack

- Swift / SwiftUI
